Title: Practice
Syntax: "practice" , "practice [skill name] [amount]"  
 
You can view the amount of Practice Points you have with "stats" or "practice" 

When you want to practice a skill, use "practice [skill name] [amount]"   
For example "practice swing 20" will spend 20 points on the swing skill.
You cannot practice any skills beyond 95.
