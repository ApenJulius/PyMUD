Title: Stats
Syntax: "stats" 

View your character stats
Body: Your physical capabilities, often the stat with most impact on Physical Damage
Mind: Your mental capabilities, often the stat with the most impact on Magical Damage
Soul: Your social and spiritual capabilities, affects Both Magical and Physical Damage
Armor: Flat damage reduction from Physical Damage
Marmor: Flat damage reduction from Magical Damage
Level: Your current level
Practice Points: whatever man
To view your skills, use the "skills" command.
To view ALL skills including those you have not learned yet, use the "practice" command without any arguments.
