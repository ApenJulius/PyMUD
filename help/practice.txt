Title: Practice
Syntax: "practice" , "practice <skill name>" 
 
You can view how many practice points you have both by using the "stats" command and "practice" command.

When you have atleast 1 practice point, you can use the "practice" command to learn a skill.
Or further advance in your knowledge of an already learned skill.

You gain one practice point whenever you level up.
And you cannot practice any skills beyond 95.
